What we do differently

St. Augustine is the dominant grass type in Central Florida, and it has opinions. Cut it too short and you stress it. Skip a week in summer and you're at 6 inches. We know the correct mow height by season and by variety — Floratam, Palmetto, and Seville all have different tolerances.

Every visit includes mowing, edging along driveways and sidewalks, and blowing clippings off hard surfaces. No half-cut jobs, no grass left on your driveway.

Common questions

Good questions, straight answers

What grass type do you work with?

Primarily St. Augustine (Floratam, Palmetto, Seville) which covers most Orlando-area homes. We also mow Bahia and Zoysia. If you have something less common, let us know when you request the estimate.

Do you service commercial properties?

We focus on residential properties — single-family homes and smaller multi-family. For large commercial accounts, we're probably not the right fit.

What's included in a standard mow?

Mow, edge (driveways, sidewalks, bed borders), and blow-off every visit. Weed eating along fence lines is included. Trimming hedges or pulling weeds from beds are separate services — ask when you get your estimate.

Do you skip weeks if it hasn't grown?

Yes. If we show up and the lawn doesn't need cutting — usually in January or February — we'll skip it and charge you nothing. We're not going to run a mower over an already-short lawn to bill you.
