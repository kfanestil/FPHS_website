Most drywall repairs are straightforward — a doorknob hole, a nail pop cluster, a crack at the corner of a window frame. What separates a good repair from a bad one is texture matching. Orlando homes run the full range: orange peel, skip-trowel, knockdown, smooth, and everything in between. We identify what's on the wall before we start and replicate it, not approximate it.

Water damage gets a different approach. If a leak caused the damage, the source needs to be fixed first — and we'll tell you plainly if that's a plumbing issue that should get addressed before we touch the wall. Once the area is dry, we cut back to clean material, set new backing or a patch panel depending on the size, tape, mud, and texture in coats. The patch is left primed and ready to paint, not just puttied over.

Common questions

Straight answers.

Can you match my existing texture?

Yes — that's the most important part of the job. Before we quote, we identify the texture type and confirm we can match it. Orange peel and knockdown are the most common in Central Florida and we replicate both. If it's a custom or unusual texture, we'll tell you upfront before we start.

What if the damage was caused by water?

We won't patch over wet or still-active damage. If a leak caused it, the source needs to be fixed first. We can handle the plumbing repair if there is one, then circle back to the wall. If we assess and find moisture still present, we'll flag it before touching anything.

How long does a drywall repair take?

Most single-area repairs — a hole, a crack, a water stain — are done in one visit over a few hours. Larger repairs requiring multiple mud coats may need a second visit for sanding and texture once the first coats dry. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we assess.

Do you paint after the repair?

We can. The standard repair leaves the area primed and ready for paint. If you want a full paint-to-match finish, add it to your estimate request and we'll include it in the quote. We match color using a paint chip or existing wall sample.
