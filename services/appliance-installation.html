Common questions

Good questions, straight answers

Which appliances do you install?

Dishwashers, over-the-range (OTR) microwaves, garbage disposals, refrigerator water lines, dryer vents, and range hood venting. We don't do gas line work — water heaters and gas appliances that require new gas connections need a licensed plumber or HVAC tech.

Do you haul away the old appliance?

Yes. Haul-away is included — just tell us when you request the estimate. We disconnect, remove, and take it with us. We don't leave old units curbside unless you specifically ask us to.

Do any of these installs require permits?

Appliance swaps — replacing like-for-like — typically don't require permits in Orange County. New circuit work or new rough-in plumbing does. If we identify anything that crosses that line, we'll tell you before we start and won't do unpermitted work that requires one.

Does it matter what brand or model?

No. We work with any brand. If you haven't bought the appliance yet and want a heads-up on anything that tends to be harder to install (unusual drain routing, non-standard mounting hardware), send us the model number first — we're happy to flag it.
